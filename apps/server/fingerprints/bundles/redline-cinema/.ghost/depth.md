## Composition

Hairlines and brightness create depth. Presence comes from photographic
atmosphere, 1px hairlines, dark-grey brightness steps, and occasional dark
gradients — never shadow stacks.

**Dark elevated plates.** Dark-grey panels sit one brightness step above the
canvas, separated by 1px hairlines rather than shadow, with concise copy and
images or large numerals as the focal point. Use them for profile, driver, spec,
and feature plates where a region must lift off the stage without breaking the
near-black mood.

**Editorial dark-to-body pacing.** Long pages move from a dark cinematic opening
into tighter editorial body sections, with large 96–128px breaks between acts, a
broad editorial container, and tighter 24–48px spacing inside cards and rows.
Depth is felt through this rhythm of brightness steps and breathing room.

**Shadow restraint.** Surface shadows stay inert; overlays make image text
legible, not decorative glass, and shadow tokens are reserved for transient
overlays — popovers and modals — only. Return to near-black for framing.

These plates frame the [cinematic image](cinematic-image) and host the
[large-number spec](spec-system) values; the only deliberate break in the
brightness-led calm is the [single red livery band](livery-band).
