# Redline Cinema Intent

Redline Cinema exists so agents can compose source-agnostic luxury-performance surfaces without falling into generic SaaS, generic motorsport dashboards, or literal automotive brand mimicry.

The language is cinematic before it is transactional: full-bleed image fields, near-black atmosphere, restrained sans type, sharp rectangular furniture, scarce red voltage, and generous editorial pacing establish the surface. Dense catalog, booking, dealer, service, or comparison tasks may switch to white sheets, but those sheets should still feel edited and premium rather than marketplace-generic.

This fingerprint was informed by a user-provided luxury-performance automotive design analysis and public automotive web research. That research is evidence, not output. Generated surfaces should not reuse any real automotive brand's names, emblem or shield marks, racing-team identity, model names, slogans, proprietary photography, or licensed font names unless the user independently supplies them.

The portable examples under `examples/core/` are derived agnostic exemplars created for this bundle. They are visual evidence for composition only: hero hierarchy, spec grids, listing sheets, event rows, heritage pacing, and rare red bands.

Treat the fingerprint as a marketplace-ready draft after exemplar wiring and validation. Treat future official screenshots, browser captures, or generated outputs as candidate evidence until reviewed and copied into the bundle in source-agnostic form.
