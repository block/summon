description: Generated surfaces must not reuse any real publisher's brand identity — they are source-informed, never source-branded.

Signal Stream is informed by public editorial sources but must stay neutral and
fictionalized. Reject the generated surface if it reuses any **real publisher's**
identity:

- a real publication name, wordmark, or masthead;
- a real author or byline name;
- a verbatim source headline, article title, or section label;
- ad creative or sponsored-module copy treated as content.

The fingerprint informs **composition only** — lead stacks, latest rails,
segmented tabs, saturated tiles, masthead *placement*. Identity content must be
fictional, user-supplied, or a task-derived title (e.g. Signal Brief, Launch
File). A masthead is a composition role (display scale + placement), never copied
letterforms or a real mark.
