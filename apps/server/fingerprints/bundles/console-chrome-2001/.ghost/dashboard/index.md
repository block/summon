## Composition

A dashboard is a packed control-panel faceplate. Reach for this surface when the
user's first question is "what is happening across this system, and what can I
act on?" — a console homepage, a status dashboard, a portal of many modules — not
when the job is to announce one product (that is the [launch](../launch)), to
browse and find items in a collection (that is the [directory](../directory)), or
to complete a single form or tool (that is the [utility](../utility)).

**Dense module faceplate.** The compact fixed-canvas density is the point:
modules should feel like controls packed onto a faceplate. Combine hero, lists,
thumbnail grids, search, polls, badges, and rails when the request supports
multiple affordances; use 2–16px internal rhythms for chrome details and 16–24px
seams between modules. Let labels and bevels prevent clutter — do not create
luxury whitespace to solve hierarchy.

**Stacked news / list rows.** Use compact platinum rows with a leading icon or
category stamp, a bold chrome-indigo or carbon headline, and a trailing
signal-orange chevron chip lit by `--chrome-led-glow`. Part the rows with
`--chrome-divider-dotted` rather than a plain hairline, and give each row plate
the hard `--chrome-bevel-hard` seam so it reads as bolted in. Use rows, not large
cards, when scanning several updates or options; keep row text short.

**Right action rail.** Stack carbon action slabs textured by
`--chrome-halftone-carbon` above a white explainer box, amber tab beaded with
`--chrome-led-pip`, poll, promo plate, calendar, or compact form in a narrow
right rail, making secondary actions feel like console-side controls. The whole
page must reveal what can be clicked, searched, read, or advanced at a glance.

The rows and rails compose the [command and navigation system](../command-nav)
and the [beveled plate and chrome system](../plates); every module earns a
section-label and status stamps from the [badge and section-label
system](../badges); row chevrons and rail buttons follow the [control
system](../controls).
