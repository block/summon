## Composition

A brief reports and routes. Reach for this surface when the user's first question
is "what happened, and what do I do?" ‚Äî an alert investigation, an incident
write-up, a status report, a single-recommendation summary ‚Äî not when the job is
to argue a product value (the [landing](../landing)), operate ongoing work (the
[workspace](../workspace)), or weigh several options against shared criteria (the
[comparison](../comparison)).

**Summary‚Üíevidence‚Üíaction.** Open with current state or the recommendation in
quiet sans. Place the proof immediately after ‚Äî
[terminal evidence](../terminal-evidence): logs, agent steps, diffs, findings.
End with one calm off-white next action from the [control system](../controls).
State the claim first; do not bury it under prose, and do not open with a
marketing posture.

**Muted severity.** Failure, warning, or blocked states stay muted unless
severity truly demands stronger semantic color ‚Äî a brief is a calm report, not a
red alarm dashboard. Carry the investigation timeline as
[hairline rows](../tiles), each scan-friendly.
